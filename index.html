The Technology

Designed for everyday autonomy.

Precision-engineered to be an extension of your own hand, offering unmatched control and stability.

Reduces Grip Requirement

By securing utensils directly to the palm, the OmniWrap significantly reduces the grip strength required to eat, making it ideal for individuals with limited hand dexterity.

Integrated Grip Retainer

Engineered with internal support that ensures the base of your utensil rests firmly against the brace. This provides maximum leverage and stability with every bite.

Patented Multi-Aperture

Our unique, patented design features interchangeable disks and multiple entry points, allowing you to find the exact utensil angle that fits your personal comfort.

Quick Start Guide

Four steps to the perfect fit.

01

Secure the Wrap

Wrap the device around your hand first. The tension should be snug enough to prevent slipping, but loose enough to maintain total comfort during your meal.

02

Insert Utensil

Once the brace is comfortably on, simply slide the handle of your spoon, fork, or knife through the specialized precision openings.

03

Find Your Fit

The brace features multiple entry points. Experiment with different openings and utensil angles to find the fit that provides you with the best leverage.

04

Align for Stability

Ensure the base of the utensil is resting firmly against the brace's internal support. This alignment unlocks maximum control.

Care & Materials
